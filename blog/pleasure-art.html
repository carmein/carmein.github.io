Pleasure Art

Featuring the clit, the G-spot, and unicorns

Posted by Michaela Carmein on June 15, 2016

Portrayed below is the tiny collection of watercolor drawings I made for my class Sexuality in Western Culture.

Mythological G

This piece, Mythological G, portrays a cross section of female anatomy. The unicorn is located where diagrams often label the G-spot to be. If the G-spot really does exist, then for some women, it's probably as awesome as unicorns. If the G-spot doesn't exist, well, then it's as mythological as unicorns.

Pleasure Spread

This piece is simply a pattern of clits and unicorns. The unicorns representing the G-spot.
